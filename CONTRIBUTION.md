# Contribution Guidelines

Thank you for your interest in contributing to FiloShop!

## How to contribute

1. **Fork the repository** and clone to your local machine.
2. **Create a branch** for your feature or bugfix.
3. **Commit your changes** with clear messages.
4. **Push to your branch** and submit a Pull Request.
5. Wait for review & feedback!

## Issues

- Please open issues for bugs, suggestions, or questions before starting major work.
- When submitting a PR, link it to the relevant issue when possible.

## Code Style

- Follow existing patterns in language, formatting, and structure.
- Write tests for new features and bugfixes.

## Communication

- Be polite and constructive in reviews and discussions.
